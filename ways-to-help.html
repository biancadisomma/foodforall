Ways to Help

Donate

Make a Financial Donation

Monetary donations allow us to purchase fresh produce, hygiene items, and other essentials. Even a small contribution goes a long way toward fighting food insecurity. Your support helps provide families with meals and essentials they need

E-Transfer Donation

Send your e-transfer to: donate@campusfoodbank.ca

Contact Us

Call us to arrange a large donation or discuss a monthly pledge:
(416) 555-1234

Donate Food

We accept non-perishable food items such as canned goods, pasta, rice, cereal, and snacks. You can drop off donations at our campus collection bins or bring them directly to our food bank office. Every item help someone in need.

Our Current Top 5 Most Needed Items

  • Peanut Butter
  • Canned Tuna
  • Clean Water
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables

Volunteer With Us

Join our team of dedicated volunteers! Help us sort donations, organize events, or assist during distribution days. Volunteering is a great way to give back, meet new people, and make an impact on campus.

Spread the Word

Join the Conversation

Following and sharing our posts dramatically raises awareness about student food insecurity. Your click helps us reach more students who need support.
