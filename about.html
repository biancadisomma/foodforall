About Us

We are a student-led food bank dedicated to ending campus hunger with dignity, care, and community support.

Student-Run Initiative

Supporting Students. Building Community.

Being a college student comes with many challenges—long commutes, long study hours, and financial pressure. One of the most overlooked issues is food insecurity. We’re here to change that.

Who We Are

Food For All is a student-led initiative at Centennial College providing nutritious food and essential supplies to students in need. Our goal is a campus where no one is left behind because of financial hardship.

“We’re more than a food bank — we’re a community of care.”

Our Values

Respect and dignity are at the heart of everything we do. Asking for help should never be a source of shame.

  • CompassionKindness and confidentiality
  • SustainabilityMindful, low-waste operations
  • CommunityStudents helping students

What We Do

We run distribution days, coordinate donation drives, and host workshops on nutrition and budgeting, partnering with local groups to bring fresh produce and essentials to campus.

Our Mission

To create a hunger-free campus by combining compassion, collaboration, and advocacy—so every student has access to healthy, dignified meals.
