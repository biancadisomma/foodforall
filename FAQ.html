How We Can Help

Below you’ll find answers to our most frequently asked questions. If you still need help, please reach out through our Contact page.

Who is eligible to use the Student Food Bank?

All currently registered students (part-time or full-time) at Centennial College are eligible. We understand that financial hardship can affect anyone.

Is my use of the Food Bank kept confidential?

Absolutely. Your privacy is our top priority. All information is kept strictly confidential and will not be shared with professors, administration, or other students.

How do I sign up or register for the Food Bank?

You can register online through our intake form. You’ll be asked for basic information (like your name and student ID) to verify enrollment — no financial documents are required.

What do I need to bring when I come for my food?

Please bring your student ID for verification and your own reusable bags, backpack, or cart to carry your items home.

How often can I visit the Food Bank?

Students can access a food hamper once every two weeks. This ensures we have enough supplies for everyone who needs assistance.

What if I have dietary restrictions (e.g., vegetarian, halal, allergies)?

We do our best to accommodate dietary needs. When you register or place an order, you can note any restrictions, and our volunteers will provide suitable items when available.

What are your operating hours and location?

Centennial College Story Arts Centre, Room 226.
Open: Tuesdays & Thursdays, 1 PM – 4 PM.
